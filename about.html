Data Sources

API

NewsAPI

Our primary data source is NewsAPI, a comprehensive news aggregation service that provides access to articles from over 80,000 news sources and blogs worldwide. NewsAPI ensures that we deliver:

  • Real-time Updates: Fresh news articles updated every 15 minutes
  • Reliable Sources: Content from verified and trusted news publishers
  • Global Coverage: News from multiple countries and languages
  • Categorized Content: Articles organized by topics for easy browsing
  • Search Capabilities: Advanced search functionality across all articles

All news content is provided by NewsAPI and remains the property of the respective publishers and authors.
